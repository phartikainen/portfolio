• Mehiläinen

    The appointment scheduling system of Mehiläinen was outdated and they needed a new, flexible system which would work in both desktop and mobile.

    Description

    The Mehiläinen Group is the best-known provider of private health care and social services in Finland. Currently, Mehiläinen's nationwide service network of privately funded health services consists of a total of 27 medical centres, occupational health centres which augment the service network, and 9 hospitals.

    Starting point

    The old appointment scheduling and nearly all the benchmarked services had step-by-step funnels. Instead of settling to status quo, we decided to do go the extra mile and do a completely new approach to shorten the path to successful online reservation. A lot of effor was put in special case handling – the basic scenario was relative simple, but all the exceptions to the flow were designed carefully as well.

    The Outcome

    The reservation flow of the appointment system was redesigned in cooperation with Mehilainen crew – both for private and occupational-health customers.

    Instead of having an infexible 5-step reservation funnel like their competitor the system was designed to be flexible to filter search results based on customers' preferences and in a map view. The appointments can be scheduled on a single page after clicking the booking button.

  • Matkahuolto

    Description

    Matkahuolto provides bus services that cover the entire country of Finland via a comprehensive system of bus terminals comprised of Matkahuolto service outlets and their equivalents, as run by certified Matkahuolto agents and other joint partners.

    Starting point

    The Outcome

    The goal was to make journey planning as straightforward as possible with a native app for frequent and occasional trips. To reduce extra input the app utilised both location data and recent trips. The frequent trips were designed to be accessed with a single click via customised home screen.
