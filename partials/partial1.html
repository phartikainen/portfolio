• BetterDoctor

    BetterDoctor needed help designing an easy and attractive way of signing up new doctors to the service.

    Description

    BetterDoctor is a comprehensive doctor search tool that brings transparency to healthcare. The company was founded in 2011 and since the launch in late 2012 BetterDoctor web and mobile apps have helped millions of patients to find the right doctor.

    Starting point

    BetterDoctor needed help designing an easy and attractive way of signing up new doctors to the service. They lacked an in-house designer, so they also preferred an user interface update on their website.

    The Outcome

    I designed the flow, user interface and graphics for their doctor sign-up in close cooperation with the CEO. I also made brand new views for their main website, such as doctor profiles and front page. The new interfaces improved the perception of the service and helped the company to sign up more doctors on their way to give the best experience for doctor search and ranking in the US.

    So far this has been the only design by me that has been featured live on Fox :)
